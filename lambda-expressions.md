Lambda expressions
==================

-   lambda-expression:

    -   params **=\>** body

    params:

    -   [ direction ] identifier

        **(** [ param-names ] **)**

    param-names:

    -   [ direction ] identifier [ **,** param-names ]

    direction:

    -   **out**

        **ref**

    body:

    -   statement-block

        expression
